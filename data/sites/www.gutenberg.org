<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Welcome"> Welcome </span></h2>

<p><b>Project Gutenberg</b> offers over 42,000 free ebooks: 
choose among free epub books, free kindle books, download them or read them online.
</p><p>We carry high quality ebooks: All our ebooks were previously published by <i>bona fide</i> publishers. We digitized and diligently proofread them with the help of thousands of volunteers.
</p><p>No fee or registration is required, but if you find Project Gutenberg useful, we kindly ask you to <a href="/wiki/Gutenberg:Project_Gutenberg_Needs_Your_Donation" title="Gutenberg:Project Gutenberg Needs Your Donation">donate a small amount</a> so we can buy and digitize more books. Other ways to help include <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.pgdp.net">digitizing more books</a>, <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://librivox.org">recording audio books</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Gutenberg:Contact_Information#Electronic_Mail" title="Gutenberg:Contact Information">reporting errors</a>.
</p><p>Over 100,000 free ebooks are available through our <a href="/wiki/Gutenberg:Partners,_Affiliates_and_Resources" title="Gutenberg:Partners, Affiliates and Resources">Partners, Affiliates and Resources</a>.
